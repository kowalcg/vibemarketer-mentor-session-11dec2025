# How I Built Two Apps With AI as a Non-Coder Founder

**Live Presentation:** https://kowalcg.github.io/vibemarketer-mentor-session-11dec2025/

A 90-minute interactive presentation for the VibeMarketer Community (December 11, 2025) documenting the journey from mechanical engineer to app builder using AI tools.

---

## üìñ About This Presentation

This is **not a tutorial on e-commerce tactics**. This is a story about business transformation through AI for entrepreneurs, marketers, and business owners who want to build software without traditional coding.

### What You'll Learn:

1. **The Journey** - How business changed from $50K+ development quotes to building apps yourself
2. **Real Apps** - SunUp (sun safety) and RunMate Pro (injury prevention) with live App Store examples
3. **Honest Obstacles** - What actually breaks, how often, and how to push through
4. **5-Step Framework** - Practical system to build your first AI app
5. **AI Toolchain** - Which tools do what (VibeCode, Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT, MCPs)
6. **Business Impact** - How apps create competitive advantages and accelerate marketing

- **App Store:** https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/sunup-by-geartop/id6751246960
- **Business Impact:** Increased perceived value, direct customer engagement, educational content delivery

### RunMate Pro
- **Purpose:** Injury prevention and recovery tools for runners, integrated with athletic tape business
- **App Store:** https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/runmate-pro/id6751238787
- **Business Impact:** Product ecosystem integration, expert positioning, new revenue opportunities
