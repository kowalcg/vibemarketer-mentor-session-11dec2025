# Welcome & The Journey: From Engineer to AI-Enabled Entrepreneur

## Overview

This section introduces Greg Kowalczyk's transformation from a mechanical engineer to an AI-enabled entrepreneur who built two live apps without traditional coding experience. It sets the stage for understanding how AI tools have democratized software development and transformed business capabilities.

## The Personal Story

### Background: Greg Kowalczyk

**Professional Journey:**
- **1990s:** Last coding experience - Visual Basic class
- **2011:** Left corporate after 20+ years as a mechanical engineer (managed teams of 70+ engineers)
- **2014:** Started e-commerce businesses (GearTOP, TapeGeeks) with daughter
- **2025:** Began building apps using AI tools
- **Current:** 2 live apps in App Store, 3 more in development

**Key Quote:**
> "Building apps was never in the life plan. But as an entrepreneur, I adapt to new tools that help me serve customers better. This is just the natural evolution."
